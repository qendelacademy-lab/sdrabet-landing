Features

Full control. Full peace of mind.

Everything you need to keep your children safe, focused, and accountable — all from one dashboard.

Screen time limits

Set daily limits per app or across the whole device. When time is up, the screen locks automatically.

App blocking

Block any app by name. Schedule blocks around school hours, mealtimes, or bedtime — every day, automatically.

Location tracking

See where your child is in real time with full location history — wherever they are.

Web filtering

Block inappropriate websites and content categories. Keep browsing safe without cutting off the internet entirely.

Usage reports

Daily and weekly summaries of how your child spends time on their device — for informed family conversations.
